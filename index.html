Our mission

Give every lawyer, consultant, and agency a digital assistant that automates the routine — not the legal judgment.

BCA is a next-generation AI platform for the legal and immigration services industry — a single digital assistant that handles client intake, helps select services, and processes payments, while qualified professionals stay in charge of the legal advice.
